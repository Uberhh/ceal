About

This online curriculum focuses on the following key aspects:

  • Addressing Social and Economic Needs: The curriculum is designed to meet both social and economic demands.
  • Problem-Posing Education: Drawing from real-world contexts and student feedback, the curriculum promotes problem-posing education as a central approach.
  • Translanguaging: Students have the freedom to choose the language they prefer for learning.
  • Engaged Pedagogy: Reflecting the principles of engaged pedagogy, the curriculum upholds freedom and equality in the learning process.
  • Multimodal and Digital Literacies: The curriculum highlights the importance of multimodal and digital literacies for a well-rounded education.
