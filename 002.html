At A Coffee Shop

Introduction: You are an experienced barista at a coffee shop inside an international campus of a university. Every day, you will interact with customers from diverse linguistic and cultural backgrounds. Your responsibilities include taking orders, discussing customer preferences, selling products, listening to feedback, answering questions, and handling complaints. Meanwhile, your supervisor has informed you that, due to a staff shortage, the shop will be recruiting new baristas. As a result, you will be responsible for training them, many of whom may also come from different linguistic and cultural backgrounds. However, there is one major challenging. YOUR ENGLISH SKILLS ARE WEAK...

This online curriculum, therefore, is designed to address your challenges. By completing this segment, we are confident that you will overcome the difficulties presented above.
